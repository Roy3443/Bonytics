About Bone Age Predictor

Bone Age Predictor is a cutting-edge AI tool designed to assist medical professionals in diagnosing and monitoring bone growth and health. By analyzing hand X-rays, the application provides accurate bone age predictions, assesses growth plate closure, and evaluates bone density.

Our mission is to empower healthcare professionals with advanced, reliable, and easy-to-use tools for better patient care.
